Burning Airlines

Flight Booker built on Backbone.js and Rails

Project Blueprint

A couple of friends and i decided to build an entire Flight Booking and Creation App from Scratch (Kinda like JetStar). The goal was to include the ability to create flights, display flights and book seats through AJAX.

User Experience

The UX is very fluid and easy on the eyes. Its a very simple flow from flight creation to flight booking. There is a nice Mouse-Hover feature implemented during the seat selection
